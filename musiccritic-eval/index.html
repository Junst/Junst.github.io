MusicCritic Human Evaluation

Welcome! This is the human evaluation portal for MusicCritic: Self-Improving Music Generation via Agentic Reasoning.

We have 3 evaluation sets covering different aspects of the MusicCritic system. Each set uses blinded A/B comparisons with Quality MOS, Match MOS, and Preference ratings.

Please use headphones and evaluate in a quiet environment. Each evaluation takes 5-35 minutes depending on the set.

Evaluation Sets

1. Feature vs Audio Critic

23 pairs ~25-35 min

Compares samples selected by the feature-based critic (BPM/key/spectral analysis) vs the audio-based critic (MuQ perceptual model). Covers 10 genres across 3 random seeds.

2. MusicGen Backend

6 pairs ~10-15 min

Tests MusicCritic with MusicGen-Large as the generation backend. Compares baseline (first candidate) vs critic-selected (best candidate) across 6 genres.

3. Multi-Prompt Robustness

4 pairs ~5-10 min

Tests robustness of the critic across different text prompts for the same genre. Compares baseline vs critic-selected outputs for hip-hop, pop, and rock with alternate prompts.

Rating Criteria

Criterion1 (Poor)3 (Acceptable)5 (Excellent)
Quality MOS
How good does the music sound overall?		 Unpleasant, noisy, incoherent Decent, listenable but unremarkable Excellent production, enjoyable music
Match MOS
How well does the music match the genre description?		 Completely wrong genre/mood Partially matches, some elements off Perfect genre match, all elements present
Preference
Which sample do you prefer overall?		 A is better / Tie / B is better
Note: BPM accuracy is evaluated automatically — please judge subjective quality and overall impression only.
