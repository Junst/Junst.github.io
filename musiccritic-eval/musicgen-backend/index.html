MusicCritic Evaluation — MusicGen Backend (6 pairs)

Thank you for participating! This evaluation compares music samples generated by the MusicGen backend: baseline (first candidate) vs critic-selected (best candidate chosen by MusicCritic). It takes approximately 10-15 minutes. Please use headphones if possible.

Instructions:
  1. Listen to both Option A and Option B for each pair
  2. Rate each option on Quality MOS and Match MOS (1-5 scale)
  3. Select your overall preference (A, B, or Tie)
Note: BPM accuracy is evaluated automatically — please judge subjective quality and overall impression only.
