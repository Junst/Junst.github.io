MusicCritic Evaluation — Feature vs Audio Critic (23 pairs)

Thank you for participating! This evaluation compares music samples selected by two different critic modes: Feature-based vs Audio-based. It takes approximately 25-35 minutes. Please use headphones if possible.

Instructions:
  1. Listen to both Option A and Option B for each pair
  2. Rate each option on Quality MOS and Match MOS (1-5 scale)
  3. Select your overall preference (A, B, or Tie)
Note: BPM accuracy is evaluated automatically — please judge subjective quality and overall impression only.

Rating Criteria

Criterion1 (Poor)3 (Acceptable)5 (Excellent)
Quality MOS
How good does the music sound overall? (production, coherence, musicality)		 Unpleasant, noisy, incoherent Decent, listenable but unremarkable Excellent production, enjoyable music
Match MOS
How well does the music match the genre description?		 Completely wrong genre/mood Partially matches, some elements off Perfect genre match, all elements present
Preference
Which sample do you prefer overall?		 A is better / Tie / B is better
